VARAZZE SURF SHOP - SUMMER/WINTER 2025 DROP

2025 // BRANDING DESIGN - GRAPHIC - DROP - ILLUSTRATION

This illustration was created for the July drop of Varazze Surf Shop. Inspired by the legend of Saint George, the design reimagines the iconic dragon with a bold and personal twist. The project involved sketching, digital illustration, and c ollaboration with the brand to bring a fresh, contemporary look to a mythological subject. The artwork was printed on limited-edition t-shirts, forming part of a larger artist-led collection. For the winter season, I also developed an exclusive illustration designed for a sweatshirt release. Inspired by the Doors of Durin from The Lord of the Rings films, the artwork draws on fantasy architecture and symbolic storytelling, translating the cinematic reference into a clean, wearable graphic.
