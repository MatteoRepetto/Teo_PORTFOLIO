SPIT IT OUT!

2020 // UX-UI DESIGN - INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE - WEB DESIGN - WIREFRAME DESIGN - VISUAL IDENTITY

“Spit it Out!” site is a common space that presents a diary as collaborative canvas: people will create a masterpiece of “words traces” by leaving messages on the website through the microphone of the computer. Step by step, at the user will be asked some questions in order to generate a “river of words” and the final result is an artwork in which all the bad and good feelings dance together.

Role
Visual identity/branding, logo design, UX-UI design.

Softwares used
Atom, p5JS, Illustrator.

Collaborators
Alessandra Crippa, Chiara Brambilla, Jessica Moreschi.
