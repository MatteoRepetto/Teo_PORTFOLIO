GROWMATE

SERVICE DESIGN // ICONS SYSTEM // VISUAL IDENTITY // GAMEFICATION

Growmate is an app designed for young VAT numbers, who need not only to be able to easily expand their social network, but also a guide to take their first steps in the world of the free market. The service therefore offers the opportunity to learn new topics about money and the management of it through a series of quizzes filtered according to the needs of the user which, once completed, will give different rewards useful for the management of their personal account (LEARN) . In addition to this, a general ranking is accessible at any time, where you can see which are the most "experienced" people near you (SHARE).

Date of realisation: 2021
Software used: Figma, Illustrator, After Effects
Project type: University - Milan Polytechnic, Visual Communication Design (Design for complex services course)
Collaborators: Elena Tonelli, Marcello Zanchetta, Zhiben Zheng
Role: Art director, Graphic designer, UI-UX design.
