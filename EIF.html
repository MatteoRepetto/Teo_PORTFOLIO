EVERYTHING IS FINE

2022 // GAME DESIGN - INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE - ASSETS DESIGN - ART DIRECTION

"Everything is Fine!" is a short single-player game for PC and Mac created in a week, during the Brackeys Game Jam of January 2022. The theme of the Jam was "It is (not) real" and for the occasion the small team of Polimi students, (called "PixelMug") decided to create and develop a graphic-adventure. in this "totally normal" slice of life game, the player has to complete simple tasks of the daily routine (such as washing, having lunch, washing dishes, ..) through point and click mini-games before the time runs out. The game was a great success, ranking 34th out of 1617 games presented, obtaining the highest score in the graphic / visual sector (4,286 / 5 points).

Role
Art Director, Technical Artist, Environment designer, UI designer

Softwares used
Asesprite, Photoshop, Unity.

Collaborators
