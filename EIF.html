EVERYTHING IS FINE

ASSETS DESIGN // ART DIRECTION // ENVIRONMENT DESIGN

"Everything is Fine!" is a mini-game for PC and Mac created in a week, during the Brackeys Game Jam of January 2022. The theme of the Jam was "It is (not) real" and for the occasion the small team of Polimi students, (called "PixelMug") decided to create and develop a short graphic adventure. The player will have to discover the mysteries that are hidden in the game world, completing simple tasks of the daily routine (such as washing, having lunch, washing dishes, ..) through simple point and click mini-games. The game was a great success, ranking 34th out of 1617 games presented, obtaining the highest score in the graphic / visual sector (4,286 / 5 points).
