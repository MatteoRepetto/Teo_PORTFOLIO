KAIFŪ - GAME UX/UI

2025 // UX/UI DESIGN - CONCEPT ART - USER RESEARCH - DESIGN SYSTEM

Kaifū is a fictional video game project I designed as a UX/UI case study, blending my passion for games with visual storytelling. The title meaning sea breeze in Japanese, explores a fantasy world inspired by the landscapes of Liguria, my home region, reimagined through the lens of Japanese culture, hip-hop influences, and anime aesthetics. In this project, I present the creative process behind the game's user experience and interface design: from early wireframes to the final prototype. I also include concept art and visual explorations that shaped the project's mood and identity.

DISCLAIMER: Some of the images used in the UI were AI-generated, and the final personas' template were reviewed with AI. For a detailed overview of which ones, please refer to the “Figma Overview” link at the bottom of this page.

Role
User research, UI design, prototyping.

Softwares used
Figma, Photoshop, Chat GPT.
