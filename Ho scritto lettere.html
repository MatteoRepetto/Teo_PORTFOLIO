HO SCRITTO LETTERE

HO SCRITTO LETTERE PIENE D'AMORE

2021 // PHOTOGRAPHY - EDITORIAL DESIGN - VISUAL STORYTELLING - COMPOSITION

“Ho scritto lettere piene d’amore“ collects some of Ungaretti's famous poems about World War I. For each there is a photograph, which metaphorically summarizes the contents. The photographs are central to the project, as they are work produced for a photography course. Matches are present in each photo and represent the precariousness of soldiers in the trenches. The font chosen is meant to recall the letters typed.
