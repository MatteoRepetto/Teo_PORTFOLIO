MAGIC THE GATHERING TOKENS

2024 // CARD DESIGN - ILLUSTRATION - LAYOUT DESIGN - DIGITAL ART

Tokens made for the card game “Magic the Gathering.” In addition to personally taking care of the illustrations, I also customized the layout of the cards, both front and back. They are divided into three sets, which differ from each other in style and concpet: the first set are Ligurian folklore-themed tokens made for the Savona Pauper League, followed then by the “Golden age” set (inspired by tarot aesthetics) and “Journey” (inspired by backroom fiction).

Role
Illustrator, Layout design.

Softwares used
Clip studio paint, Procreate.
